## Visiting the Lab

Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory is located on the North Shore of Long Island, approximately 35 miles east of New York City.

### Directions

**By Car:**
- From NYC: Take the Long Island Expressway (I-495) to Exit 44N (Route 135 North), then follow Route 25A East to Cold Spring Harbor.
- The main entrance is located at 1 Bungtown Road.

**By Train:**
- Take the LIRR from Penn Station to Syosset station.
- Taxi service is available from the station to the laboratory.

**By Air:**
- The nearest airports are Long Island MacArthur Airport (ISP), JFK International Airport, and LaGuardia Airport.
